Text-based Logic Game


A 10,000 line long text based logic game.
Written in Python, the game is based upon Raymond Sullyan's The Lady and the Tiger.
Having various different story lines (The Island of the Knight or Knaves, Transylvania, Prison Escape, and the Asylum), each with their own distinct puzzles and soundtracks.


As an added feature, the character could acquire money to purchase items, health potions, and other items depending on which setting the game was on.
